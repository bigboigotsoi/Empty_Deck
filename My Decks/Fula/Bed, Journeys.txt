	
# BED AND TRAVELS: #


# Travels:

> I'll be ready soon!
- Mi parranin joh!

> I'm ready!
- Mido parrah!

> I'm not ready yet.
- Mi parraani tawon. 

> I'm coming.
- Mido arrah.

> I'm closeby.
- Mi batteemah.
- Mi yototoh.

> I want to drink.
- Mido faalah yarr deh.

> I want to go.
- Mido faalah yaah deh.

> I'm busy!
- Jungoh am goh di nangi!


# Bed Time:

> I'm going to Bed.
- Mi waaloeemah.

> I'm not going to Bed.
- Mi waalohyaaki.

> I woke up at 7 (Full time in fula).
- Wat-toh jeh-didi pin me.

> He/She is awake.
- Mbo fini.

> He/She isn't awake yet.
- Mbo finaani tawon.

> Mom is sleeping.
- Daa di daani.

> It's tiring.
- Di tampehreh.
