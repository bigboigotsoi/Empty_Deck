	
# FULA PHRASES: #

# Colours

> Colour.
- Kulor.

> What two-part phrase do most colours end with?
- Air-joh.

> Black.
- Bal-air-joh.

> White.
- Don-air-joh.

> Blue.
- Bul-air-joh.

> Red.
- Bod-air-joh.

> Yellow.
- Net-air-joh.

> Green.
- Banaani.

# It's phrases.

> It's hot!
- Di wulli!

> It's hot outside!
- Toh yaas di wulli!

> It's cold!
- Di huubi!

> It's cold outside!
- Toh yaas di huubi!

> It hurts.
- Di muusah

# Others:

> Well/Good
- Komuji

> Better
- Bullorri

> Rubbish (informally)
- Nyama-nyama

> One person
- Neddoh gortoh
