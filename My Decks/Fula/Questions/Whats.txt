
# FULA QUESTIONS 1: #



# What Questions...

> What is this?
- Hodum wonin?

> What is that?
- Hodum wondum?

> What do you think?
- Hodum mi jiitdah?

> What are you doing?
- Hodum mbat-taanin?

> What are you watching?
- Hodum darrtah?

> What do you want?
- Hodum paaleh dah?

> What did you cook?
- Hodum deftah?

# > Will you cook soon?

> What are you cooking?
- Hodum defat?

> What are you eating?
- Hodum nyamtaanin?

> What have you eaten?
- Hodum nyamtaa?

- Hono oh weetay?

> What's this called?
