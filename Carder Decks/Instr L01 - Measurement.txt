
# 

> What is Instrumentation?
- The tools used to measure, monitor and control mechanical processes or parameters.

> Main purposes of measurement?
- Regulation/Inspection 
- Condition Monitoring
- Feedback Control

> Why is Regulation/Inspection measurement important?
- To stay within trade/industry quality or health and safety specs.

> Why is Condition Monitoring measurement important?
- To allow users to apply necessary actions before, during, or after the natural phenomena occurs.

> Why is Feedback Control measurement important?
- To maintain accuracy and and performance of computer controlled operations.
